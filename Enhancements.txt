Enhancements

1. Examine Object
Allows the player the player to examine items they find, revealing hidden messages leading to bonus items, or too the end  of the game.

2. Examine Cup
Allows the player to examine item they find, revealing hidden messages to bonus items or to the end of the game.

3. Examine key 
Allows the player to examine a key which unlocks a second floor lab in DH where you find deers eating a computer lab.

4.Eat Donut
 Allows the player to use one of the items they picked up and giving them nutrition to bring them closer to the end of the game.

5.Drink Coffee
Allows the player to drink Coffee to stay awake for the exam and brings them closer to the end of the game.
